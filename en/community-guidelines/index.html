Community Guidelines for Link2Wink

These Community Guidelines supplement the Terms and Conditions of Link2Wink. They describe the rules for respectful, safe, and honest interaction within the app.

What Link2Wink is about

Link2Wink brings real people together for real-life encounters. Respect, honesty, and safety are at the core.

Our community should be a place where people feel comfortable, can communicate openly, and are never put under pressure.

Real-life connection before chat

Link2Wink is not a traditional chat-based dating app. The idea is simple: The first contact happens in real life.

The chat is a bridge – a second chance. Not a substitute for real-life interaction.

We value:

  • real people instead of personas
  • current and authentic photos
  • openness instead of mind games
  • the courage to take the first step instead of endless messaging

Link2Wink is designed to help you take that first step – nothing more, nothing less.

Respect & communication

Treat others the way you would like to be treated.

This means:

  • a friendly and respectful tone
  • respecting boundaries
  • accepting a “no”
  • no pressure, no manipulation, no insults

Showing interest is welcome. Being intrusive is not.

No harassment, no pressure, no spam

The following is not allowed:

  • repeated messages without a response
  • pressuring others for meetings, phone numbers, or photos
  • mass or copy-paste messages
  • emotional pressure or guilt-tripping

Each person decides how much contact they want.

No fake profiles

Only real people with genuine intentions.

The following is not allowed:

  • stolen or AI-generated images
  • false identities or intentionally incorrect age information
  • significantly outdated or misleading photos
  • multiple accounts or attempts to bypass bans

Authenticity is the foundation of trust.

No minors

Link2Wink is intended exclusively for individuals aged 18 and over. Profiles of minors will be removed immediately.

Protection of minors

We enforce a zero-tolerance policy regarding any content or behavior involving or endangering minors.

In particular, the following is prohibited:

  • creating, sharing, or requesting content that sexualizes or exploits minors
  • impersonating a minor
  • seeking or initiating contact with minors
  • manipulating, deceiving, or engaging minors in inappropriate interactions

Violations will result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to the relevant authorities.

We encourage all users to report any suspicious content or behavior immediately.

No commercial content

Link2Wink is not a marketplace or advertising platform for users.

The following is not allowed:

  • advertising or sales offers
  • escort, sugar dating, or other commercial services
  • spam or automated messages

The app is intended for personal encounters – not business purposes.

No illegal content

Content or behavior that violates applicable law is not permitted.

This includes, in particular, offensive, discriminatory, threatening, hateful, pornographic, violent, or otherwise unlawful content.

Safety in real-life meetings

Real-life encounters are the core of Link2Wink. Safety always comes first.

We recommend:

  • meeting in public places
  • informing a trusted person
  • listening to your instincts
  • ending a meeting at any time if it does not feel right

No one owes anyone a meeting. Safety always comes before politeness.

Photo verification – trust without pressure

Photo verification is optional and helps build trust in real profiles. It is not mandatory – but a sign of openness.

Reporting system

Inappropriate, boundary-crossing, or suspicious behavior can be reported at any time.

Reports are reviewed confidentially. They serve to protect all users.

Consequences of violations

Violations of these rules are handled fairly but consistently.

Depending on the severity, measures may include:

  • notice or warning
  • restriction of features
  • temporary suspension
  • permanent account deletion

Serious violations may result in immediate suspension.

Violations are assessed at the reasonable discretion of the provider.

There is no entitlement to use the platform. The provider reserves the right to remove content and to restrict or delete user accounts in the event of violations of these guidelines, the Terms and Conditions, or applicable law.

All measures serve one goal: A community where people feel safe, respected, and welcome.
